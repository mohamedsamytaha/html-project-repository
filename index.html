What is Artificial Intelligence? How Does AI Work?

       Artificial intelligence (AI) is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned
with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human
intelligence. AI is an interdisciplinary science with multiple approaches, but advancements
in machine learning and deep learning are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector
of the tech industry.
